Summer Overnight Camp


The training camp is set to run for four days and three nights spanning July 25-28. This event is perfect for players looking to take their game to the next level with plenty of on-court and off-court training and principles, as well as significant live-match competition elements. Those wishing to opt out of the overnight portion of the camp can sign up for the commuter registration beginning at $399. The standard registration costs $460 and includes three nights of accommodations in Ithaca College dorms and associated meals.

Registration Opens January 1

Summer Positional One Day Camp


The one-day camp is schedule to take place on July 23rd. Participants will have the option to pick from participating as a Libero, Middle/Outside Hitter, or Setter. Training drills and other events will be tailored to the individual position groups with a high-level of one-on-one individual instruction. Cost of the one-day event is $99.

Registration Opens January 1
