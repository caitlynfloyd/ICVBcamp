Ithaca College Volleyball Camp


The Ithaca Volleyball Academy is back for its second year: perfect for anyone serious about taking their game to the next level. There will be lots of on-court instruction in addition to building blocks in other settings. New this year will be video analysis and the use of a radar gun during our serving training. During the competition segments, you'll be able to test yourself and your new skills against the other athletes in the camp. Or how about the battle between the campers and the coaches? This camp includes 3 nights in the dorms on campus, all meals, and the best athlete:coaches ratio in the country. You'll receive an Ithaca Volleyball Academy shirt for the tournament on the last day.


Camp starts on Thursday afternoon at 3pm (registration opens at 1:30pm) and graduation in the gym will take place at 11:30am on Sunday. The last morning, friends/family/fans are invited to come watch as we play a competition-style challenge session.
